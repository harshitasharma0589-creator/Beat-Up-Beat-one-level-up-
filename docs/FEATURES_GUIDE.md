# Beat Up ✨ — Feature & Guide Sheet
**Theme:** "Beat one level up"

Welcome to the **Beat Up** experience! This application is designed to elevate your productivity by structuring your tasks ("Beats") as a personalized audio mix. By combining dynamic voice analysis, failure-prediction models, and personalized coaching modes, you'll never let your rhythm fall flat.

---

## 🚀 Core Features

### 1. Chronological Audio Dictation (Voice-First Engine)
- **Natural Narration:** Speak or type exactly what you are trying to accomplish, mentioning the current time of day or urgency.
- **Auto-Timeline Mapping:** The AI engine classifies your task into separate chronological segments: `Morning Beats`, `Afternoon Beats`, `Night Beats`, or `Anytime Freestyle`.
- **Granular Division:** Your blocker is parsed, and structured into hyper-detailed sequential subtasks so you can immediately find your start line.

### 2. Personalized Coaching Decks (Multiple Modes)
Switch your vibe instantly from your control panel:
- **DJ Champ (Athletic Coach & DJ Mode) 🥊:** High-octane rhythm coaching and athletic boxing metaphors to push your velocity.
- **Friendly Sidekick (Friend Mode) 🤝:** Warm, empathetic, non-stressful validation. Reminds you that you are doing great and guides you with gentle tea-break intervals.
- **Executive Advisor (Professional Mode) 💼:** Structured milestone reports, critical path optimization, and strict analytical guidelines.

### 3. Fail-Safe Predictive Models (Plans A, B, and C)
When you describe your blocker, the engine predicts your risk of failure (`low`, `medium`, or `critical`). To prevent failure, it automatically generates three backup layouts:
- **Plan A (The Perfect Track):** The ideal, high-standard focus plan if everything goes perfectly.
- **Plan B (The Halftime Remix):** The fallback sequence if you get interrupted, tired, or lose 50% of your focus window.
- **Plan C (The Bass Drop):** The absolute minimum viable execution plan to get partial credit or save a zero day right before the buzzer.
- **One-Click Injector:** Instantly inject any of these backup layers directly into your task board with a single tap.

### 4. Dynamic Audio Visualizer & Dancing Tunes 🎵
- **Dancing Waveforms:** Located in the *Mixer Metrics* slide, experience an interactive visualizer where different colorful tunes (pink, orange, rose, white) dance up and down to your active BPM.
- **Tempo Modulation:** Real-time speed modulation changes the oscillation speeds based on whether your current blocker requires high-tempo emergency crunch (140 BPM) or chill focus (60 BPM).

### 5. Level & XP Progression Board 🏆
- **XP Integration:** Master subtask drills to score **15 XP** and feed your overall level progress.
- **Energy Meter:** Monitor your live mental energy meter which shifts dynamically from full charge to warning states.
- **Streak Tracker:** Keep your daily jam streak alive with persistent session memory.

### 6. Clean Multi-Slide Architecture 🎛️
To prevent visual clutter, the layout is divided into distinct, elegant slide decks:
1. **🔊 Consultation Desk:** Keep your AI consultations, coach selection, and failure plan analysis first and center.
2. **🎛️ Mixer Metrics:** Watch your progress meters, level badges, and live dancing color visualizers.
3. **📅 Beat Timeline:** Track, expand, and finalize your chronological subtasks, or assemble your own custom drills.
